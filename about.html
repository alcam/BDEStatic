Our Mission

At Best Day Ever Coffee & Crepes, we believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to showcase their unique talents and contribute meaningfully to their community.

Our mission is to create an inclusive workplace that empowers adults with disabilities, providing them with valuable job skills, confidence, and a sense of purpose – all while serving delicious coffee and crepes to our wonderful community.
